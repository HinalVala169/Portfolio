About Me

I am a dedicated Unity Game Developer with a strong background in both 2D and 3D game development. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the Institute of Technology Aditya Silver Oak, and a Diploma in Computer Engineering from L.J Polytechnic. Over the past 2.5 years, I have honed my skills in crafting dynamic gameplay, integrating APIs for real-time game mechanics, and creating visually appealing interfaces. My journey includes significant contributions to games like 'Candy Mania,' 'Blackout,' 'Scratch It,' and 'Juntos,' developed during my tenure at Codehabit and Yudiz Solutions Pvt Ltd. My technical skills include proficiency in Unity3D, C#, HTML and CSS. I am also experienced in using tools like Visual Studio, Git, and GitHub.
